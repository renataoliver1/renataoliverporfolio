TYPOGRAPHY COMPARISON

Luxury Positioning vs. Generic Output

✘ GENERIC APPROACH
Style & Fashion

Using unrefined or overused fonts (like Arial or basic system fonts) fails to create a unique brand identity. The spacing is inconsistent, and the "voice" feels amateur.

✓ LUXURY POSITIONING
Style & Fashion

Using high-contrast serifs with intentional letter spacing creates an immediate sense of exclusivity. This is the "Vogue" effect—it signals authority and high-value content.
