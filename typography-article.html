TYPOGRAPHY IN DIGITAL STRATEGY

The Psychology of Typeface Selection

Typography is the primary vehicle for brand voice. In a world where luxury is often defined by minimalism, the choice of a typeface carries the weight of a brand’s entire history. For my personal brand, I have chosen to pair the classic authority of a high-contrast Serif with the clean, democratic efficiency of a modern Sans-Serif.

A Serif typeface, like the one used for the "Renata Oliveira" header, creates a sense of establishment and premium positioning. It mimics the traditional mastheads of high-fashion magazines, instantly signaling to the viewer that the content they are about to consume is of high value.
